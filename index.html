About Me

I have done PhD in life science and worked in academic research for some years. During this time I acquired essential skills some of them are critical thinking, data analysis, communication and collaborative within team members. More recently I have worked with NSH Scientific Private Limited which is a service based life science company in India. As a co-founder and an active member in management team, I had been involved in dealing with clients, planning and managing projects and consulting the clients as per the obtained results. By working in NSH Scientific Pvt. Ltd., I gained skills of management, negotiation with clients and wrap up different tasks in a single project.

As situation arises in last two years during the pandemic I moved back to Germany because my wife is working here and started to think to change my career. In my last company I worked with our web developer and some of my family members are experience software engineer. It piqued my interest to become a web developer.

Currently I am working in Deutche Post as part time which enhances my German skills. Apart from it I am learning frontend technologies from online resources. I already learned some essential concepts of HTML, CSS, Javascript and Github.
